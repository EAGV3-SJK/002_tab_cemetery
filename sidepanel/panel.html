Welcome to Tab Cemetery

This extension reads titles and short snippets from your open tabs, sends them to Google Gemini, and groups them into themes.

What is sent to Gemini

  • Tab title, URL, and up to 500 characters of visible page text.
  • Nothing from chrome://, extension pages, or PDFs.
  • Nothing from domains you add to the exclude list.
  • Nothing at all if you enable Privacy Mode.

You will need a free Gemini API key from Google AI Studio.

Settings

Stored locally via chrome.storage.sync. Get one at AI Studio.

Tabs on these domains appear in the list but are never sent to Gemini.

Saved sessions

Each session is a snapshot of tabs. Opening does not auto-open tabs — it shows a list.
